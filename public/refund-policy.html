Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Webora, we prioritize transparency and fairness. Please read our refund policy carefully.

1. Advance Payments

All advance payments are non-refundable once the project has started.

2. Project Cancellation

If a project is canceled by the client after work has begun, no refunds will be issued for completed or ongoing work.

3. Service-Based Nature

Due to the nature of digital services, refunds are generally not applicable once delivery or partial delivery is made.

4. Exceptional Cases

Refunds may be considered only in rare and exceptional circumstances at Webora’s sole discretion.

5. Contact

For refund-related queries, contact us at weborastudiozz@gmail.com.
